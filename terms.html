Refund & Cancellation policy As a policy we would require an advance payment of Rs. 1000 per guest for a table of 4 or more to confirm your reservation. We will send you a link to your preferred mobile number and email, to initiate the advance payment. Once we have received the advance, you will receive a confirmation email with details of your reservation. Please note that this amount will be adjusted against your final bill. If the booking is cancelled within 24 hours prior to the time of table reservation– the advance will be forfeited. If cancelled prior to 24 hours from the time of table reservation, no charges apply.
